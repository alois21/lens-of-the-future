About Us

From Bootcamp to Fellowship — where young filmmakers learn to tell their stories, master their craft, and reclaim Africa’s narrative through film, media, and technology.

In 2025, we launched the first-ever Lens of the Future Bootcamp — a groundbreaking program that brought together Emmy®-nominated filmmakers, creative mentors, and 30 young Zimbabwean storytellers. Now, we’re evolving into a full Fellowship — nurturing the next wave of African filmmakers to tell our stories, on our own terms.

About Lens of the Future

Lens of the Future is a filmmaking and storytelling fellowship dedicated to training, mentoring, and empowering young African creatives to tell their own stories — powerfully, truthfully, and on their own terms.
